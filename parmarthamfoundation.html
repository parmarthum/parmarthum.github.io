Who We Are?

Parmartham Foundation is a national level non-profit organization working for the welfare of underprivileged children and women in the field of Education, Healthcare and Livelihood.

Read More

Vision

Parmartham Foundation envisions contributing significantly towards quality education, healthcare and livelihood facilities to the socially and economically disadvantaged people.

• Education

Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world and the most effective way to break out of the cycle of poverty

• Health

Our health constitutes suffering and deprivation of the most fundamental kind andTaking care of health is our sole responsibility

• Livelihood

The 'Parmartham Foundation towards Fostering Women's Empowerment in Eastern Uttar Pradesh, India' aims at addressing the gaps that hamper the progress of women,

• Women Empowerment

Women's empowerment is about creating high-level corporate leadership for gender equality.
